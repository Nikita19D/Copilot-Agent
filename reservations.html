Reservation Policies

Confirmation

Once you submit your reservation request, you will receive an email confirmation within 30 minutes. If you don't receive a confirmation, please contact us directly.

Cancellation

We kindly ask that you notify us at least 24 hours in advance if you need to cancel or modify your reservation. For parties of 6 or more, we require 48 hours notice.

Late Arrival

We will hold your reservation for 15 minutes past your reservation time. If you're running late, please call us to let us know, and we'll do our best to accommodate you.

Seating Preferences

While we will do our best to accommodate seating preferences, specific table requests cannot be guaranteed. Thank you for your understanding.
